Message from Principal

Dear Parents,

For little children,play is learning and learning is play.Therefore besides teaching,a child needs a friendly loving environment where he can explore discover himself and enjoy his childhood,Tender Buds has an environment,full of love vibrancy and freedom so that learning becomes a enjoyable experience.

This should be your approach at home as well.As parents,create a loving positive atmosphere at home to help your child blossom.Your child is at stage where you can shape his personality.Where love and appreciation can help him come out with flying colours pressure and nagging can mar his own personality.
